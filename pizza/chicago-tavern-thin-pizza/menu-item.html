Ingredient Amt
Flour (12.5-13.5%)400g
Water (Room Temp)220g
Yeast1g
Sea Salt8g
Veg Oil20g
Sugar10g

Tavern Thin

Thin crust party cut is crisp but won't break with sauce and charred cheese at the edge. An homage to Vito & Nick's on Pulaski St in South Chicago.

Instructions

  1. Combine flour, sugar, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Dissolve yeast in water, then add to dry ingredients.
  3. Add oil and mix until dough forms.
  4. Knead for 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  5. Let dough rise, covered, for 1-2 hours or until doubled in size.
  6. Preheat oven to 500°F (260°C).
  7. Roll out dough thin, place on a pizza screen or pan.
  8. Top with sauce, cheese, and desired toppings.
  9. Bake for 8-10 minutes until crisp and golden.
  10. Cut into squares and serve.
